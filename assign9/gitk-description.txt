gitk-description.txt

Upper Left Window: Shows the commit history with all past commits. They 
are labeled by the a short commit message.

Upper Middle Window: Shows the name and the email of the author of the commit.

Upper Right Window: Shows the date and time for the corresponding commit.

Bottom Right Window: Shows the files that were altered in the commit.

Bottom Left Window: Shows author, committer, parent, branches, child,
and commit message. Below that is each file that was changed in the commit, and
shows what changed through a diff file format.
